## Why Discord Webhook Manager?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Visual Message Editor" icon="paintbrush">
    Design Discord messages with a real-time preview that matches Discord's appearance exactly. Support for embeds, mentions, custom colors, and all Discord message components.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Automation & Scheduling" icon="robot">
    Schedule messages for specific dates and times, or set up recurring messages (daily, weekly, monthly). Attach files that are automatically cleaned up after sending to save storage.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Team Collaboration" icon="handshake">
    Invite team members to collaborate on webhooks with role-based permissions (Admin, Editor, Viewer). Share templates and manage access across your organization.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Content" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Generate message content automatically using OpenAI GPT or Google Gemini. Perfect for creating engaging announcements, updates, and notifications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
